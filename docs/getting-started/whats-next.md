# You're up and running

If you've followed all six steps, cli_debrid is installed, connected to your media server, and actively monitoring your content sources. Items should be flowing through the queue.

---

## Confirm everything is working

1. **Dashboard** — check that your debrid provider and media server show as connected (green status)
2. **Queues** — items from your content sources should appear in the Wanted queue and progress through Scraping → Adding → Collected
3. **Library** — after a Plex/Jellyfin scan, collected items appear here

If something isn't working, check the **Logs** page for errors or ask on [Discord](https://discord.gg/ynqnXGJ4hU).
